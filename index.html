DaVinci Resolve

DaVinci Resolve is a professional-grade video editing software developed by Blackmagic Design. It combines video editing, color correction, visual effects (VFX), motion graphics, and audio post-production all in one application. It’s widely used in Hollywood films, TV shows, YouTube videos, and commercial productions.

CapCut

CapCut is a free and easy-to-use video editing app developed by ByteDance. It offers powerful tools like video trimming, transitions, filters, text effects, background removal, and AI-powered features such as auto captions. Ideal for both beginners and content creators.

VN

professional video editing app designed for both beginners and advanced users. It's especially popular among vloggers, creators, and mobile editors who want more control and features than basic editors offer
